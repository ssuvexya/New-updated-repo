About Us

Happy Paws-Pet Adoption Center is a kennel club and adoption center located in Kathmandu. Our goal is to rescue stray dogs as well as abandoned dogs in the streets of Kathmandu. Happy Paws is a very spacious in kennel area and takes very good care of dogs in need of a home. We take good care of abandoned and stray dogs and feed them 4 times a day. our shelters are very animal friendly. You can visit us and contact us at: happylittlepaws40@gmail.com
